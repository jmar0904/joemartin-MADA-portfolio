About Me

I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2016. Having studied Philosophy and Journalism, my interests were aligned in media and publishing. Following graduation, I joined a start-up that uses natural language processing to review fiction manuscripts at scale. Throughout my time here, I was mainly in charge of developing content for our website and social media, conducting customer research, and making sales. However, I became more interested in the technical side of the company.

I moved to Athens in 2018 and I’m currently the Assistant Director of Admissions Operations for Georgia’s Full-Time MBA program. I work behind the scenes managing our CRM/applicant database and building digital resources for our students. I occasionally emerge from my office to help recruit new students.

In addition to my full-time role in Terry, I’m also pursuing my MS in Business Analytics. I’m primarily interested in process management and mining for data to understand how end-users engage with online content.

I’ve been working in R for a couple of years now, primarily analyzing data related to our applicants and our office’s workflow. Additionally, I’ve completed coursework in SQL and Python.
